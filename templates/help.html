Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DeepGuard AI.

DeepGuard AI is an Artificial Intelligence based Cyber Threat Prediction System that detects, predicts and analyzes cyber attacks.

Login to the Dashboard → Open Prediction Page → Click "Choose File" → Upload your CSV dataset → Click Predict.

Currently DeepGuard supports CSV (.csv) datasets for threat prediction.

The Machine Learning model analyzes uploaded network traffic data and predicts possible cyber attacks based on trained patterns.

Go to the Reports page and click Download PDF or Download Excel.

Administrators, Security Analysts, Researchers and Students can use the system.
